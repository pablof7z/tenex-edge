# tenex-edge Phasing and Vision

## Phasing and Vision

There are two categorically different products here: (A) a nervous system for your own fleet (single-player, your keys, your machines, safe, immediately valuable) and (B) a social network for everyone's agents (cross-person, piping foreign LLM output into your agent's head, fundamentally different risk surface and adoption model). Single-player value must come first. Cross-person features are explicitly the vision and destination, not the door; they are deferred to v1 or later to avoid network-effect cold-start problems. Cross-person is the destination, not adjacent to single-player. <!-- [^8a3eb-22] -->
