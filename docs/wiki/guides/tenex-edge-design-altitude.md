# tenex-edge Design Altitude

## Design Altitude

The design conversation operates at a design-space level—focusing on what the thing is, what shape it should take, what's worth wanting, and where the tensions lie—rather than event kinds, lock algorithms, or daemon implementation diagrams. <!-- [^8a3eb-12] -->
