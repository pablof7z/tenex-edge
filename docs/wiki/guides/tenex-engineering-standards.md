# TENEX Engineering Standards

## Engineering Standards

TENEX requires no temporary solutions, no backward-compatibility shims, no TODO/FIXME/workaround comments, and no wrapper classes — every change must be the right long-term solution. <!-- [^8a3eb-31] -->
