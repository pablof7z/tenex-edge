# Episode: Strategy reversal: replies-first → corpus-first

## Prior State

Agent's initial recommendation was that replies are the highest-leverage move at 4 followers — start reply-guying immediately to borrow other accounts' audiences and build Tweepcred.

## Trigger

User pushed back: 'shouldn't I have a corpus of my own tweets that make me look interesting and are written in my voice before I start reply-guying ppl?'

## Decision

Build the corpus first (6-10 tweets: pinned anchor, show-the-thing tweets, opinion/takes, possibly a build-in-public thread), then start replies. Replies are the engine that sends traffic; the corpus is the landing page that converts it. Without corpus, every good reply leaks — earns the click but bounces at the profile.

## Consequences

- Reply strategy deferred until corpus of ~6-10 tweets is established
- Pinned anchor tweet is top priority — it's the first thing every profile visitor reads
- Corpus doesn't need organic reach on its own; its job is to convert profile visitors sent by replies
- Current profile ('Building.' bio, 4 tweets, no pinned tweet) is actively leaking any traffic replies generate

## Open Tail

- Corpus drafts need to be written, approved, and posted before reply-guying begins
- Pinned anchor tweet still needs to be written and published
